WSG Facts

Facts about some efforts into the Web Sustainability Guidelines (WSG) to make the web work better for all people and the planet.

92 Guidelines covering UX, Web Development, DevOps, & Business / Product Strategy.

254 Success Criteria to meet the guidelines on various aspects of sustainability.

160 advisory Techniques provided to meet Success Criteria.

2,000+ Resources (URLs) to assist with implementation of guidelines.

150+ contributors from over 20 nations around the world.

Iterated upon on by 48 participants (including 27 Invited Experts) representing 15 organizations in the Sustainable Web Interest Group.

The draft Web Sustainability Guidelines (WSG) are under development.
